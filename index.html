FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We have got answers.

Who should attend this Business Meetup?

This event is designed for entrepreneurs, startup founders, small business owners, freelancers, consultants, and corporate professionals looking to expand their network and learn from industry leaders. Whether you are just starting out or running an established business, you will find immense value.

What should I bring to the event?

Please bring a valid photo ID, your digital ticket (sent via email after registration), and plenty of business cards. We recommend bringing a notebook or tablet for taking notes during sessions. A laptop is optional but useful for the networking sessions.

Is parking available at the venue?

Yes, the Grand Convention Center offers both underground parking (500+ spaces) and valet service. Parking is complimentary for all attendees. We also recommend nearby public transit options — the venue is a 5-minute walk from the Downtown Metro Station.

Can I transfer my ticket to someone else?

Absolutely! Tickets are fully transferable up to 48 hours before the event. Simply email us at support@businessmeetup.com with the new attendee's details, and we will update the registration at no extra cost. Transfers within 48 hours may incur a small processing fee.
